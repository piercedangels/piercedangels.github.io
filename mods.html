About US

Pierced Angels is a Multi National community of alt models spanning across the globe including models from USA, Canada, Australia, The UK and Europe.
Our models are highly body-positive and real professional, they are all fully xcanada either Nationally or Internationally and have worked with some the best photographers around the world.
Together we pride ourselves on our ability as a community to create positive mindset and business relationships with Photographers, Brands and Magazines alike.

Background

It is open non-profit community with broad perspective and vision. We are always looking for people to join and help to create better world.
It started from single instagram page who helped pierced and alt girls to fight with insecurities and fears. Now it is grown with thousands of followers and willing to expand.
